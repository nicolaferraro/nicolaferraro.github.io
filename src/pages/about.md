I'm a principal software engineer at **Red Hat**, writing open source software for fun. They also pay me for that.

I'm a committer and PMC member on [Apache Camel](https://github.com/apache/camel). I'm also co-author of [Apache Camel K](https://github.com/apache/camel-k): a lightweight platform for running Apache Camel integrations on Kubernetes and OpenShift that leverages Knative for "serverless" features.

I've worked on the [Syndesis](https://syndesis.io) integration platform (based on Apache Camel) and on the development tools of [Fabric8](https://github.com/fabric8io/fabric8), such as the Fabric8 Maven Plugin for building and running Java applications on Kubernetes and OpenShift.

You can find me on [GitHub](https://github.com/nicolaferraro) and [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/nicolaferraro/). My full CV is on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/nicolaferraro/).
